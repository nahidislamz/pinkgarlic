About

Pink Garlic Restaurant

Pink Garlic specialises in contemporary modern Indian cuisine and brings its culinary principles to Taunton. The interior reflects its vibrant modern atmosphere and its vivid designs has a generous use of rich warm colours to make you feel at home. Pink Garlic aim is to provide a high standard of service and excellent cuisine to all our customers. Our fully trained skilled and efficient staff will do their outmost to make your visit enjoyable and a memorable experience you will want to repeat. Here at the Pink Garlic we use the best selection of fresh ground spices to create mouth watering flavours to suit all taste buds. In this carefully created menu each dish has prepared using the right quantity and appropriate number of spices to create this delectable menu. Our chef has used his vast years of experience to create this menu for your pleasure.
